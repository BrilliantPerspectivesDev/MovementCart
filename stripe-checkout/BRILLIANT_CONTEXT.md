## Guiding Leadership Principles:

1. **From Church to Movement**. We are upgrading from being a church to being a movement of Kingdom activators. 
2. **One Thing**. To simplify this movement, we are combining BrilliantPlus as part of the movement.  
3. **Level of Membership:**
    1. **Member**. $47 per month giving you access to 
        1. **The App**: BrilliantPlus
        2. **Gatherings**: Monthly Gatherings (First Monday of the Month 4pm PT)
        3. **Small Groups**: Online weekly small groups via zoom. Join based on the time that is convenient to you. 12-Week studies from BrilliantPlus app like:
            1.  Fruit of the Spirit, and
            2. The Nature of Freedom. 
        4. **Events**. Get free access for all current members. This includes:
            1. Limitless life with God training events.
            2. Annual movement conference: Brilliance26 which is a 3-day virtual event held in the first quarter of each year. 
    2. **Ambassador**.  If you love this movement and want to promote it, you can join the Ambassador Program. This gives your the following benefits:
        1. You have all same benefits as a Member, AND
        2. You can participate in promotion of the movement by providing people a link to access the BrilliantPlus app. They will enjoy a 5-day free trial and if they join as a member you share in a portion of the membership fee.
        3. You will receive Ambassador-only monthly training on how to grow your impact and income.
        4. You will receive a Brilliant Debt Card to access your funds. 
        5. The Annual Fee for being part of the Ambassador Program is $10. 
        6. Currently, the Ambassador Program is only being offered in the U.S.A with the intention of offering other countries access in the future. 
        7. We love the idea of people being able to provide for themselves, their families, or their ministries as they help us grow impact and income. 
    3. **Partner (Not to be included on sales pages)**. Gifts to the movement at any level which are deductible and enable the Movement to move quicker. All these gifts are tax deductible for all U.S. taxpayers. There are several membership levels.
